Journey to a Healthier Self: The Role of Sports and Fitness in My Life

You may be surprised to find this section on my website, but it serves as a testament to the important role sports have played in shaping who I am today.

Team sports from a young age taught me valuable lessons in discipline, companionship, responsibility, and leadership. Though I had to set it aside during the first few years of my career, returning to indoor soccer in university showed me the benefits of physical activity not only on my mental health, but also on my academic performance.

Three years ago, I shifted my focus towards fitness and gym training. However, as someone who has always been driven to compete and challenge myself in sports, I decided to start training for OCR (Obstacle Course Racing) events like the Spartan Race, which demands both strength and endurance.

With the aim of continuing to push myself physically and mentally, my current focus is on triathlons, a sport that allows me to constantly test my limits and improve my performance. In just four months of training, on October 2022, I won the Barcelona Triathlon in the SuperSprint distance (350m swim, 10km bike ride and 2.5km run). This season I am challenging myself to compete in the Sprint distance (750m, 20km, 5km), keeping in mind the objective of progressing and gradually working towards the ultimate ambition of participating in an IRONMAN event that involves a 4km swim, a 180km bike ride, and a 42km run.
